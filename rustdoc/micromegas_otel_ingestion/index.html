OTLP/HTTP ingestion adapter for the micromegas data lake.

This crate accepts OTLP Export*ServiceRequest proto messages and writes them as micromegas blocks. Translation from proto to parquet rows happens at the analytics layer; this crate’s job is just identity synthesis (resource → process_id / stream_id), per-resource block splitting, and idempotent SQL inserts.
