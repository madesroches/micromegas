#[unsafe(no_mangle)]
pub unsafe extern "C" fn mm_flush(_handle: *mut MmHandle)
Expand description

Flush all in-memory log and metric buffers to the transport.

The transport thread will then ship them to the ingestion server. Safe to call with a null handle (operates on the global dispatch).
